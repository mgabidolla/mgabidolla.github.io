Research interests

My research focus is on Machine Learning and Optimization. My recent projects are on the topic of learning decision trees, forests and tree-based models; and their application in various domains: supervised learning, clustering, neural network compression, and model interpretability.

About

I am a 4th year PhD candidate at University of California, Merced (UCM) supervised by Miguel Á. Carreira-Perpiñán. I hold a BSc degree in Computer Science from NU in Astana, Kazakhstan. You can find my CV [here] .
